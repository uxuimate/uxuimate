Our Process

How this case study was delivered, step by step.

Eight clear delivery phases from discovery to post-launch support.

01

Discovery & Brief

Defined product goals, user needs, and success criteria for the UX redesign scope.

02

UX Research & Analysis

Audited current flows and usability issues to identify trust and navigation barriers.

03

Information Architecture

Restructured product discovery and content hierarchy to reduce cognitive load.

04

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity flow structures for key journeys including product and checkout paths.

05

UI Prototyping

Built high-fidelity interactive prototypes in Figma for realistic user validation.

06

Iteration Cycles

Refined layouts, labels, and interactions through multiple design-feedback rounds.

07

Usability Testing

Validated revised journeys with users to improve confidence and task completion.

08

Design Handoff

Delivered final UX/UI files with clear components, annotations, and implementation guidance.
